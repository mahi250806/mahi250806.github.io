About

I'm a B.Tech Computer Engineering student at D. J. Sanghvi College of Engineering, pursuing honors in Financial Engineering. My work sits where quantitative finance meets machine learning — portfolio construction, volatility forecasting, and risk.

Right now I'm interning at Century IQ Solutions, building a multi-asset risk engine that applies Hierarchical Risk Parity and Black–Litterman allocation across equities, commodities, and ETFs. I use ML to generate return and volatility views that feed the Black–Litterman posterior, and I'm extending the pipeline with GARCH and LSTM models to forecast volatility dynamics.

Outside of allocation, I've built ML systems for fraud detection on highly imbalanced data and full-stack platforms with Django and React. I care about models that are honest about their limitations — the assumptions, the failure modes, and what I'd test next.
